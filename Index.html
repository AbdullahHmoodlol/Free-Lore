FNAF Lore: A to Z

Welcome, brave soul. Here lies a cursed glossary of Freddy-laced horrors.

A - Afton
The infamous William Afton — killer, father, and glitchy boogeyman.
B - Bite of '87
A frontal lobe incident that left fans theorizing for decades. Chomp!
C - Circus Baby
Animatronic child-snatcher with a voice smooth as trauma syrup.
D - Discs
Those weird sound illusion discs from the books. Hallucinations, baby.
E - Elizabeth Afton
Daughter of William Afton. Got too close to Baby. Oops.
F - Fazbear Entertainment
The shady company that just keeps reopening haunted pizzerias.
G - Golden Freddy
A mysterious suit. Is he Cassidy? Is he revenge? Yes.
H - Henry Emily
Co-creator of the animatronics. Tragic dad. Final burner.
I - Immortality
Because dying once isn’t enough in this franchise.
J - Jeremy Fitzgerald
The night guard in FNAF 2. The one who possibly got bitten.
K - Kids
Too many lost souls. Too many suits. Too many tears.
L - Lure
How Afton led the children to their doom. And still got hired.
M - Michael Afton
William’s son. Sister Location survivor. Walking corpse. MVP.
N - Night Shift
Minimum wage. Maximum hauntings. No dental.
O - One Bite
That's all it took. The Bite of '83, anyone?
P - Puppet
Possessed by Charlie, Henry's daughter. Gives life. Also trauma.
Q - Quiz Minigames
Strangely deep lore in 8-bit minigames. Good luck decoding it.
R - Remnant
The soul-metal stuff that lets animatronics stay haunted. Spooky goo.
S - Springlock
Great tech. Until it rains. Then you’re soup in a suit.
T - Theories
MatPat’s kingdom. Timeline spaghetti is canon now.
U - Ultimate Custom Night
50/20 mode of pure pain. Possibly Afton's personal hell.
V - Vanessa
Security Breach’s morally ambiguous bunny-chaser. Or killer bunny?
W - William Afton
Yes, again. He always comes back. Get used to it.
X - X-Factors
All those weird unexplained ghosts, glitches, and game files. Huh?
Y - You
You're the night guard. You're Michael. You're Gregory. You're bait.
Z - Zap
From shocking animatronics to burning down buildings, FNAF always ends with a spark.

Thanks for surviving the A–Z of FNAF. Sleep with one eye open.
